About us

Invermay Vet Clinic was started in 2026 by Dr Darren Taylor at the site where Paul's Veterinary Clinic has served the community for a number of years. It is a privately-owned practice that aims to provide high-quality veterinary care with transparent pricing.

Location

Invermay Vet Clinic is conveniently located on the main road in Invermay, Launceston. The clinic is in a beautiful heritage building that previously housed Paul's Vetinary Clinic.

Story of our logo

Our logo combines the silhouettes of two of Darren's pets - one from the past and one from the present. Together, they represent the journey that Darren has taken from South Africa to be able to open his own practice in his chosen home of Launceston.

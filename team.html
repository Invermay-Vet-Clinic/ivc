Veterinarian

Invermay Vet Clinic is a small clinic owned by Dr Darren Taylor.

Dr Darren Taylor

Veterinarian

Darren has been working as a veterinarian for over 20 years across welfare and private practices in South Africa, Hong Kong, and now Tasmania. He has a special interest in orthopaedic surgery and the problem-solving opportunities it presents. Originally from South Africa, Darren has been working in Launceston since 2022 and excited to be opening his own clinic in his new home town. He looks forward to meeting you and treating your pets.

Nurses

Our friendly nursing team supports consultations, surgery, and patient care with experience, dedication, and compassion.

Jen Perkins

Veterinary nurse

Jen is a cert IV veterinary nurse with many years of experience and a special interest in canine reproduction and puppy care. She has been working at Paul's Veterinary Clinic for the past few years and is happy to be able to continue to provide continuity of care to the clients and patients she has worked with at the clinic.

Annette Hayes

Veterinary nurse

Annette is a highly experienced cert IV veterinary nurse with a special interest in surgical nursing and postoperative care. She has worked at Paul's Veterinary Clinic for the past 10 years and looks forward to continuing the relationships and high standard of care that Paul's clients have come to expect.

Georgie Barnes

Veterinary nurse

Georgie has been working as a vet nurse at Paul's Veterinary Clinic for the past few years and is skilled across the board in patient care and nursing support. Her friendly and compassionate approach to patient care is rivalled only by her nursing expertise.
